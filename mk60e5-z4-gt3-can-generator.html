Notes — MK60E5 Z4 GT3 ABS

Tools and notes for diagnosis and configuration of the MK60E5 from a BMW Z4 flashed with Z4 GT3 / Motorsport software. For diagnostics, K-Line pin 2 must be wired to the diagnostic connector (unlike normal E1/E5).

Part number: 34516769162 with 829.3 code on module. Compatible with VW yaw sensor 7H0907652A (note: different pinout than BMW yaw sensor).

When flashed, these units allow specifying tire circumference separately for front and rear, plus ABS intervention mode with 10 steps. Max to min intervention order: 0, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.
